Hello, World

I am a dedicated Computer Software Development student at the Northern Alberta Institute of Technology (NAIT), with a passion for software engineering, data and illustration. Currently, I am exploring courses that delve into the intricacies of data science, aspiring to carve a career path in this dynamic field. My ongoing pursuit of knowledge includes mastering Python for data science through the esteemed platform, DataCamp. I frequently engage with the software development community in Edmonton through Dev Edmonton meetups and local hackathons.

Over the past several years, I have honed my skills as a digital illustrator. Presently, I contribute as a writer and illustrator for The Nugget, NAIT's school newspaper, with my artwork prominently featured in their print issues. Notably, I have received recognition for my contributions, having earned a multimedia award for one of my February 2023 cover design.

I am active in the videogame production community as the President of the Game Creator Space at NAIT. I aim to foster a vibrant community for students passionate about video game development and design through events such as LevelUp. This involvement includes coordinating regular discussion meetings, organizing game jams, and implementing various initiatives to enhance the overall experience for our members.

Feel free to connect with me! I'm always eager to engage with the software development and data/machine learning community, seeking new opportunities and collaborations.
